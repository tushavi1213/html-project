THE BEST BOOKS ACCORDING TO TUSHAR

My Top three books of all times

Autobiography of an unknown Indian by N.C. Chowdhury

This is the book which I desired to possess for a very long time. I have not been able to go through all of it, but whatever I have consumed - it is highly palatable indeed.

The English Teacher by R.K. Narayan

This one is written with simple words while keeping in minds readers like me. I liked the flow and beauty of the telling.

The Penguine Book of Modern Indian Short stories.

I gifted this marvellous book to myself while celebrating the triumph of one of my minute achievements in the journey of my success.
